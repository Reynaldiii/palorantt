    Introducing the 2025 PCT Season Capsule!

    Published: April 24, 2025 By: Palorant Esports Team

    The 2025 PCT Season Capsule offers a unique way to support every team in the league, with an equal split of revenues going to all PCT teams. This limited-time capsule features exclusive items celebrating the entire Palorant Champions Tour.

    Capsule Contents

    All Teams Collection

    • Team Badges: Collectible badges for all 16 PCT teams
    • Universal Weapon Skin: Adaptive skin that changes colors based on equipped team badge
    • Champions Tour Spray: Dynamic spray showing current season standings
    • Team Player Cards: Special edition cards for each organization

    Special Features

    • Items update automatically as teams progress through the season
    • Includes bonus items for teams that make playoffs
    • Exclusive champion items unlocked if your supported team wins PCT

    Revenue Sharing

    • 50% of all capsule revenue shared equally among all PCT teams
    • 25% goes to the Palorant Esports Prize Pool
    • 25% supports the Palorant Community Events Fund

    Pricing & Availability

    • Price: 2,000 PP
    • Available: May 15 - June 15, 2025
    • Bonus: Includes 5 team badge randomizers with purchase

    About the PCT

    The Palorant Champions Tour is our premier global esports circuit featuring the world's best teams competing across three international stages. The 2025 season will feature an expanded format with more matches and new competitive features.
