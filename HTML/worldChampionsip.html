    World Championship 2023

    Published: April 19, 2025 By: Palorant Esports Team

    The biggest Palorant esports event of the year is coming to Indonesia! Top teams from around the world will compete for a $1,000,000 prize pool in this spectacular championship tournament.

    Tournament Details

    Event Information

    • Dates: May 10-20, 2025
    • Location: Jakarta International Convention Center, Indonesia
    • Teams: 16 (4 from each major region)
    • Format: Group stage into double elimination playoffs

    Prize Distribution

    • 1st Place: $400,000 + Championship Trophy
    • 2nd Place: $200,000
    • 3rd-4th Place: $100,000 each
    • 5th-8th Place: $50,000 each

    Participating Teams

    North America

    • Sentinel Gaming
    • Cloud Rebels
    • Frost Esports
    • Phoenix Rising

    Europe

    • Northern Lights

    • Valkyrie Squad

    • Brazilian Storm
    • Latin Fury
    • Middle East Falcons

    Event Highlights

    • Opening ceremony featuring Indonesian cultural performance
    • Exclusive in-game drops for viewers
    • Meet-and-greet sessions with pro players
    • Special championship merchandise available
    • Champion's skin for the winning team's MVP
