    PCT Ascended Team Bundles Now Available

    Published: April 28, 2025 By: Palorant Esports Team

    Celebrate the Ascension champions with the latest team capsule, featuring exclusive cosmetics and proceeds supporting the winning teams.

    Bundle Contents

    Champion Team Bundle

    • Team Agent Skin: Custom-designed Agent skin with champion colors
    • Player Cards: Limited edition cards for each team member
    • Spray: Championship trophy spray
    • Buddy: Miniature trophy charm

    Runner-Up Bundle

    • Team Sidearm Skin: Custom pistol design
    • Player Card: Team logo card
    • Spray: Tournament logo spray

    Availability

    • Available in-store from June 15 - July 15, 2025
    • 30% of proceeds go directly to the teams
    • Exclusive to Palorant Champions Tour participants

    Pricing

    • Champion Bundle: 2,500 PP
    • Runner-Up Bundle: 1,800 PP
    • Individual Items: 750-1,200 PP each

    About PCT Ascended

    The Palorant Champions Tour Ascended tournament featured the top 16 teams from around the world competing for a $500,000 prize pool. This year's champions, Team Phoenix, secured their victory with a stunning 3-1 finals performance.
