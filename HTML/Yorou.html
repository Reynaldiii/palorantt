    Duelist

    Duelist is a role designed to be the team's frontline attacker. They specialize in aggressive plays and are expected to secure the first kills and create space for the team. Duelists are fast, self-sufficient, and ideal for players who enjoy taking the lead in combat.

    Yorou

    Japanese native Yorou rips holes straight through reality to infiltrate enemy lines unseen. Using deception and aggression in equal measure, he gets the drop on each target before they know where to look.

    Abilities

    Fakeout
    Deploys an echo that mimics footsteps to mislead enemies.
    Blindside
    Throws a flash that bounces off surfaces and blinds enemies.
    Gatecrash
    Sends out a tether that Yorou can teleport to after a short delay.
    Dimensional Drift (Ultimate)
    Enters another dimension, becoming invisible and invulnerable to enemies.

    Fakeout

    Basic Ability

    EQUIP an echo that transforms into a mirror image of Yorou when activated. FIRE to instantly activate the mirror image and send it forward. ALT FIRE to place an inactive echo. USE to transform an inactive echo into a mirror image and send it forward. Mirror images explode in a Blinding Flash when destroyed by enemies.
