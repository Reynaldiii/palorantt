    Temple of Anubis

    Published: April 19, 2023 By: Palorant Map Design Team

    Prepare to explore the ancient Temple of Anubis, a sand-covered ruin filled with shifting traps and hidden passages, coming in Season 6. This Egyptian-themed map introduces revolutionary gameplay mechanics that will challenge even the most experienced players.

    Map Features

    Dynamic Environment

    • Shifting Sand Traps: Certain areas will periodically sink, slowing movement
    • Collapsing Walls: Destructible sections that create new pathways
    • Hidden Chambers: Secret rooms containing power-ups
    • Time-based Events: The map changes slightly each round

    Key Locations

    • Main Sanctuary: Central point with multiple elevation levels
    • Burial Chambers: Tight corridors perfect for ambushes
    • Sun Altar: Open area with rotating platform
    • Scarab Vault: High-value control point

    Gameplay Innovations

    New Mechanics

    • Sand Movement: Footprints remain visible for 10 seconds
    • Hieroglyph Puzzles: Solve for temporary map advantages
    • Anubis Blessing: Random power-up spawns
    • Time-limited Zones: Areas that become inaccessible

    Release Details

    • Release Date: August 1, 2025 (Season 5 launch)
    • Availability: Unranked for first 2 weeks, then competitive
    • Map Rotation: Will replace Canyon in competitive pool
    • Exclusive Event: "Curse of Anubis" limited-time mode
