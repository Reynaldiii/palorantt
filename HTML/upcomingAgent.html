    Meet "Vipera" - The Stealthy Infiltrator

    Published: April 12, 2025 By: Palorant Development Team

    The newest agent joining the Palorant roster in Season 5 is Vipera, a deadly infiltrator who specializes in poison-based abilities and stealth tactics. This venomous operative brings a unique playstyle to the game that will challenge both enemies and allies to adapt.

    Abilities Overview

    Tactical Ability: Toxic Dart

    Fire a poison dart that applies a damage-over-time effect to enemies. The poison spreads to nearby enemies if they stay close to the initial target.

    Signature Ability: Camouflage Cloud

    Deploy a cloud of toxic mist that grants Vipera and allies inside temporary invisibility while slowly damaging enemies in the area.

    Ultimate Ability: Serpent's Embrace

    Transform into a shadowy serpent form for 8 seconds, gaining increased movement speed, the ability to climb walls, and leaving a trail of poison that damages enemies.

    Passive Ability: Venomous Step

    Vipera's footsteps are silent, and crouch-walking leaves behind temporary poison traps that slow enemies.

    Playstyle and Strategy

    Vipera is designed as a hybrid between a duelist and controller, excelling at:

    • Flanking enemy positions with stealth capabilities
    • Area denial with poison effects
    • Disrupting enemy formations
    • Executing hit-and-run tactics

    Team Synergies

    Vipera works particularly well with agents who can:

    • Reveal enemy positions (to maximize poison spread)
    • Create choke points (to concentrate poison effects)
    • Provide healing (to counteract Vipera's low base health)

    Development Insights

    Lead Agent Designer, Maria Chen, shared some thoughts about Vipera's creation:

    "We wanted to create an agent that would encourage more tactical, stealth-based gameplay while still maintaining Palorant's fast-paced action. Vipera's poison mechanics add a new layer of strategy where timing and positioning become even more crucial."

    Balance Considerations

    The development team is closely monitoring:

    • Poison damage values in competitive play
    • Duration of stealth effects
    • Ultimate charge rate

    Release Details

    • Release Date: August 1, 2025 (Season 5 launch)
    • Unlock Method: 100,000 Palor Points or Agent Contract
    • Launch Bundle: Includes exclusive skin, player card, and spray
    • Availability: Will be disabled in competitive play for first 2 weeks
