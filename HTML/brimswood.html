    Controller

    Masters of tactical dominance, Controllers specialize in area denial and battlefield manipulation. They partition maps into zones of control using smokes, molotovs, and strategic utility - blocking enemy sightlines, restricting rotations, and creating safe pathways for their team. Perfect for players who think like battlefield commanders.

    Brimswood

    A seasoned commander, Brimswood brings precision and strength to the battlefield. With his sky smokes and incendiary abilities, he controls the map and supports his team with tactical dominance.

    Abilities

    Sky Smoke
    Deploys smoke clouds to obscure vision.
    Incendiary
    Launches a grenade that creates a damaging fire zone.
    Stim Beacon
    Boosts fire rate and reload speed for allies.
    Orbital Strike (Ultimate)
    Calls in a powerful laser strike over an area.

    Sky Smoke

    Basic Ability

    Equip a tactical map. Fire to set locations where Brimswood's smoke clouds will land. Alt fire to confirm, launching long-lasting smoke clouds that block vision in the selected area.
