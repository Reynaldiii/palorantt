    Duelist

    Duelist is a role designed to be the team's frontline attacker. They specialize in aggressive plays and are expected to secure the first kills and create space for the team. Duelists are fast, self-sufficient, and ideal for players who enjoy taking the lead in combat.

    Aeon

    A speedster from the Philippines, Aeon surges forward with lightning-fast movement and electric energy. She excels at breaking through enemy lines and catching opponents off guard.

    Abilities

    Fast Lane
    Deploys two energy walls that block vision and damage enemies passing through.
    Relay Bolt
    Throws an energy bolt that bounces once and concusses anyone it hits.
    High Gear
    Activates a burst of speed; slide once while charged.
    Overdrive (Ultimate)
    Unleashes a lightning beam that deals high damage with incredible accuracy at high speed.

    Fast Lane

    Basic Ability

    FIRE two energy lines forward on the ground that extend a short distance or until they hit a surface. The lines rise into walls of static electricity that block vision.
