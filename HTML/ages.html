    Sentinels

    Sentinels are defensive experts who focus on locking down areas and protecting their teammates. They excel at holding sites, watching flanks, and providing support through traps, heals, or information-gathering abilities. Sentinels are key to a strong defense and can slow down or stop enemy pushes effectively.

    Ages

    The bastion of China, Sage creates safety for herself and her team wherever she goes. Able to revive fallen friends and stave off aggressive pushes, she provides a calm center to a hellish fight.

    Abilities

    Barrier Orb
    Equip a wall orb. Fire to place a solid wall that can be rotated before placing. It can block paths and be used for creative plays.
    Slow Orb
    Equip a slowing orb. Fire to cast an orb that creates a field, slowing anyone who moves through it and reducing their jump height.
    Healing Orb
    Equip a healing orb. Fire to heal an ally or hold to heal yourself over time.
    Resurrection (Ultimate)
    Target a dead ally to revive them with full health after a short cast time. This can shift the tide of a round completely.

    Basic Ability
