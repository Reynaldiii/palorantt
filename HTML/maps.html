    Ascent

    Standard Map

    Map Overview

    Ascent is set in Venice, Italy, featuring open mid areas and tight choke points. The map's unique mechanic is the two mechanical doors that can be permanently destroyed.

    With its open mid area and multiple levels, Ascent rewards teams that can control space and use utility effectively to isolate engagements.

    Key Features

    • Two bomb sites (A and B) with distinct approaches
    • Mechanical doors that can be permanently destroyed
    • Vertical gameplay with multiple levels
    • Open mid area that's crucial for map control

    Strategic Tips

    For Attackers

    • Control mid to split the defense
    • Use utility to clear tight angles
    • Destroy doors to create new pathways

    For Defenders

    • Hold mid control to limit attacker options
    • Use one-way smokes effectively
    • Play retake positions if needed

    Icebox

    Standard Map

    Map Overview

    Set in a Russian Arctic outpost, Icebox features extensive verticality with ziplines and tight angles. The map emphasizes sharp aim and precise utility usage.

    With its complex verticality and multiple levels, Icebox challenges players to master both horizontal and vertical space control.

    Key Features

    • Extreme verticality with multiple levels
    • Ziplines for rapid rotation
    • Tight angles that reward precise aim
    • Narrow corridors that favor close-quarters combat

    Strategic Tips

    For Attackers

    • Use vertical space to surprise defenders
    • Control mid to access both sites
    • Clear angles methodically

    For Defenders

    • Hold unconventional angles
    • Use utility to block ziplines
    • Play retake positions if needed

    Haven

    Standard Map

    Map Overview

    Haven is set in a monastery in Bhutan and is unique for having three bomb sites (A, B, and C), forcing defenders to spread thin or make strategic decisions about which sites to prioritize.

    The map's three-site layout creates dynamic gameplay where rotations and map control are crucial for success.

    Key Features

    • Three bomb sites (A, B, and C) - unique in Palorant
    • Multiple pathways between sites
    • Central courtyard that's key for rotations
    • Garage area that's crucial for mid control

    Strategic Tips

    For Attackers

    • Split pushes to stretch defenders thin
    • Control garage for mid dominance
    • Fake attacks to confuse defenders

    For Defenders

    • Adapt your setup based on attacker tendencies
    • Play for retakes on some sites
    • Use utility to slow down pushes

    Lotus

    Standard Map

    Map Overview

    Set in India, Lotus features three sites with rotating doors that can change the flow of combat. The map rewards creative plays and adaptive strategies.

    With its rotating doors and multiple pathways, Lotus offers dynamic gameplay where control of the doors can dramatically shift round outcomes.

    Key Features

    • Rotating doors that change pathways
    • Three bomb sites with unique approaches
    • Breakable walls for new sightlines
    • Multiple levels for vertical play

    Strategic Tips

    For Attackers

    • Control door rotations to cut off defender rotations
    • Use breakable walls for unexpected angles
    • Fake attacks to draw defenders

    For Defenders

    • Use doors to quickly rotate between sites
    • Hold unconventional angles
    • Play for retakes when appropriate

    Pearl

    Standard Map

    Map Overview

    Pearl is set in an underwater city in Portugal, featuring a classic two-site layout with long sightlines and tight chokepoints. The map emphasizes fundamentals and precise gunplay.

    With its mix of long sightlines and close-quarters areas, Pearl rewards teams that can adapt their playstyle to different areas of the map.

    Key Features

    • Underwater theme with unique visuals
    • Long sightlines for ranged engagements
    • Tight chokepoints that favor utility usage
    • Multiple pathways for flanking opportunities

    Strategic Tips

    For Attackers

    • Use utility to clear long angles
    • Control mid for rotation options
    • Split pushes to stretch defenses

    For Defenders

    • Hold aggressive angles to deny space
    • Use utility to block chokepoints
    • Play for retakes when appropriate
