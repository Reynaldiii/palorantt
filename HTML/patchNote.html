    Patch 10.06 Highlight

    Published: April 25, 2025 By: Palorant Dev Team

    Patch 10.06 brings agent balance changes, bug fixes, and smoother gameplay. Enjoy improved visuals and a better overall experience!

    Agent Updates

    Sage

    • Barrier Orb (Wall): Health increased from 800 to 1000
    • Slow Orb (Molly): Slow duration increased from 4s to 5s
    • Resurrection (Ultimate): Cost reduced from 7 to 6 points

    Yoru

    • Gatecrash (Teleport): Distance increased from 30m to 35m
    • Blindside (Flash): Flash duration increased from 1.5s to 2s
    • Dimensional Drift (Ultimate): Cost reduced from 8 to 7 points

    Rank Changes

    Bronze

    • Minimum Matchmaking Rating (MMR) required for entry: 800
    • Increased match bonus for players achieving "win streaks"

    Silver

    • Small adjustments to matchmaking to reduce queue times
    • Bronze players now need more wins to be promoted to Silver

    Gold

    • Matchmaking algorithm adjusted to reward consistency in performance
    • Gold 3 players now face fewer "smurfing" issues in lower-ranked matches

    Platinum

    • Placement system reworked to consider recent performance more heavily
    • Ranked match lobbies now have faster queue times for Platinum players

    Diamond

    • Introduction of a new system for Diamond players to track individual performance statistics
    • Revised promotional system to ensure smoother progression

    Quality of Life Improvements

    • New option to customize crosshair color per weapon type
    • Added ping indicators for high latency situations
    • Improved party system with easier invite management
    • New "Avoid Player" feature for matchmaking (limited to 3 players)

    Bug Fixes

    • Fixed issue where some abilities would clip through certain walls
    • Resolved collision detection problems on Ascent map
    • Fixed rare crash that could occur during ultimate ability usage
    • Addressed audio issues with footsteps on certain surfaces
