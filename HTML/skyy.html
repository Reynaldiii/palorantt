    Initiator

    Initiators are role that specialize in breaking through enemy defenses and gathering information. They create opportunities for their team to enter contested areas by disrupting opponents and revealing their positions.

    Skyy

    Hailing from Australia, Skyy and her band of beasts trail-blaze the way through hostile territory. With her creations hampering the enemy, and her power to heal others, the team is strongest and safest by Skyy's side.

    Abilities

    Regrowth
    Heals nearby allies within a radius using a healing trinket. Cannot heal herself.
    Trailblazer
    Sends out a controllable Tasmanian tiger trinket that leaps and concusses enemies on contact.
    Guiding Light
    Deploys a hawk that can be steered. Activating it again causes a flash if aimed at enemies.
    Seekers (Ultimate)
    Sends out three seekers that track down and nearsight the three closest enemies.

    Regrowth

    Basic Ability

    EQUIP a healing trinket. HOLD FIRE to channel, Healing allies in range and line of sight. Can be reused until her healing pool is depleted. Skye cannot heal herself.
