    Palorant

    Welcome to Palorant

    Palorant is a competitive 5v5 tactical shooter where precise gunplay meets unique agent abilities. Set in a near-future world, players choose from a diverse roster of Agents to engage in high-stakes matches across global locations.

    Each match is a round-based battle where teams alternate between attacking and defending, with gameplay that rewards strategic thinking, team coordination, and precise execution.

    • Strategic team-based gameplay
    • Diverse roster of unique Agents
    • Tactical abilities that complement gunplay
    • Competitive ranked mode
    • Regular content updates

    Best Agent of the Month

    Yorou

    Yorou is a duelist from Japan who manipulates space and deception to outsmart his enemies. With the ability to open dimensional rifts, he can sneak through enemy lines without being seen.

    His playstyle is perfect for players who love flanking and confusion tactics. With blinding flashes and quick teleports, Yoru strikes from unexpected angles, turning the tide of battle in an instant.
